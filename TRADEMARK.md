## Modified Versions

If you fork or substantially modify the AML kernel, you must:

1. Choose a different name for your fork.
2. Remove all Arianna Method branding.
3. State clearly that your project is a derivative, not the original.

You do not need to remove copyright notices or license headers. The LGPL v3 requires you to keep those.
